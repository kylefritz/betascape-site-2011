Betascape Overview

Betascape 2011 is a multi-day experience designed to bring artists and technologists together to Learn and Connect to Create. Attendees will participate in one day of learning and workshopping and a one-day CreateCamp. The goal is to inspire artists and technologists to innovate at the intersection of art and technology in a whole new way. Betascape also aims to drive greater economic development for Baltimore by attracting and retaining technologists, artists and new companies. Betascape will be held from September 23 - 25th 2011 on Station North in Baltimore, Maryland, a designated Arts and Entertainment District in Baltimore City.

The event is being organized by a group of volunteers, working in either technology or web design. Our long term goal is to establish an annual event that is imparting real skills and providing new ideas and relationships for people to innovate @ the intersection of art and tech.
